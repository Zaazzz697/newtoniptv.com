## What Is an IPTV Reseller?

An IPTV reseller purchases credits or subscriptions in bulk from a wholesale IPTV provider, then sells individual subscriptions to end customers at a profit.

Think of it like buying wholesale and selling retail — but digitally, with no physical stock to manage.

---

## How Does an IPTV Reseller Panel Work?

Most IPTV reseller programmes come with a **reseller panel** — an online dashboard where you can:

- Create and manage customer accounts
- Set subscription durations (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
- Monitor active connections and usage
- Regenerate or reset credentials
- Track your remaining credit balance

You buy credits upfront (e.g. 10 credits = 10 subscriptions) and use them to create accounts for your customers.

---

## How Much Can You Earn as an IPTV Reseller?

Your profit is the difference between your wholesale cost and the retail price you charge customers.

**Example:**
- You buy 10 subscriptions at £5 each (wholesale) = £50 spent
- You sell each subscription at £9.99/month = £99.90 earned
- **Profit: £49.90 per month** from just 10 customers

Scale this to 50 or 100 customers and the numbers become very attractive.

## What to Look for in an IPTV Reseller Provider

### Reliability
Your customers will blame you if streams go down. Choose a provider with **99%+ uptime** and a robust server infrastructure.

### Channel Quality
Ensure the provider offers **4K and HD streams** across all major UK channels — sports, entertainment, news, and international.

### Support
You need fast support from your wholesale provider so you can quickly resolve issues for your customers.

### Competitive Wholesale Pricing
The lower your cost price, the more room you have to compete on retail price while maintaining healthy margins.

### Easy-to-Use Panel
A clean, intuitive reseller dashboard saves you time and reduces errors when managing customer accounts.
