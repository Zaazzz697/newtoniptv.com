## What Is Smart IPTV?

Smart IPTV refers to IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) that runs natively on a Smart TV. Instead of needing a separate device like a Fire Stick or Android box, you install an IPTV player app directly onto your Samsung, LG, or Sony Smart TV.

### Smart IPTV (SIPTV)
One of the most popular apps for Samsung and LG Smart TVs. Simple to set up — you load your M3U playlist URL and you're ready to stream. Available from the Samsung Smart Hub and LG Content Store.

Works on both Smart TVs with Android TV and as a standalone app. Supports Xtream Codes API login — making it very easy to set up with Newton IPTV credentials.

### SS IPTV
Another excellent option for Samsung and LG Smart TVs. Free to download, supports M3U playlists and Xtream Codes.

## How to Set Up IPTV on a Smart TV (UK)

Setting up IPTV on a Samsung or LG Smart TV takes about 5 minutes:

1. **Open your Smart TV's app store** (Samsung Smart Hub or LG Content Store)
2. **Search for "Smart IPTV" or "SS IPTV"**
3. **Install the app**
4. **Open the app and note your TV's MAC address**
5. **Visit the activation URL on your phone or computer**
6. **Enter your Newton IPTV M3U URL or Xtream Codes credentials**
7. **Restart the app** — your channels will load automatically

## What Internet Speed Do I Need?

For Smart IPTV to work smoothly on your Smart TV:

- **HD streams** — minimum 10 Mbps
- **4K streams** — minimum 25 Mbps
- **Multiple devices simultaneously** — 50 Mbps or faster recommended

Most UK broadband connections handle this easily. If you're on a slow connection, Newton IPTV automatically serves lower-resolution streams to prevent buffering.
